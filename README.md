📦 FMCG – Sales & Performance 

📌 Project Summary
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard created to analyze the sales and operational performance of an FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) portfolio. The dashboard transforms 3 years of raw sales data (2022–2024) into clear, visual insights that help understand how the business is performing across different products, regions and channels.
It focuses on answering key business questions such as:

Which product categories and brands are generating the most revenue?
Which sales channel — Retail, Discount or E-commerce — is growing the fastest?
Are promotions actually increasing sales volume?
Where are the stockout and supply chain gaps?


🎯 Purpose of the Dashboard
The main objective of this project was to practice real-world FMCG data analysis and build a multi-page dashboard that reflects how companies monitor their sales, promotions and supply chain performance. It demonstrates how data can be used to track growth, identify trends and support better business decision-making.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Power BI – for building the dashboard and creating visualizations
Power Query – for data cleaning and transformation
DAX – for calculated measures such as Total Revenue, Promo Sales %, Stockout Count and Delivery Gap


📂 Data Source
The dashboard is built using the FMCG_2022_2024 dataset sourced from Kaggle, which simulates real FMCG sales operations. The data includes:

190,757 rows of transactional sales data
Product categories — Milk, Yogurt, Juice, SnackBar, ReadyMeal
Sales channels — Retail, Discount, E-commerce
Regions — North, South, Central
Promotion flags, stock levels, delivery data and pricing information

This dataset allows the dashboard to replicate a realistic FMCG business intelligence scenario.

✨ Key Features
📊 Page 1 — Sales Overview

KPI cards showing Total Revenue, Total Units Sold, Avg Price Per Unit and Total Transactions
Monthly revenue trend line chart showing seasonality and growth patterns across 3 years
Donut chart showing revenue share by product category
Clustered bar chart comparing units sold vs revenue by category revealing the volume vs value gap
Treemap showing top 10 brands by revenue

🗺️ Page 2 — Channel & Regional Performance

Stacked bar chart showing revenue by region split by sales channel
Stacked bar chart showing revenue by region split by product category
Line chart tracking channel revenue growth over 3 years showing E-commerce vs Retail trends

🎯 Page 3 — Promotions & Pricing

Clustered bar chart comparing promoted vs non-promoted units sold by category
Stacked bar chart showing promo vs non-promo revenue contribution by brand
Line chart tracking average price trend by pack type (Single, Multipack, Carton) over time

📦 Page 4 — Stock & Supply Chain

Line chart comparing stock available vs units sold over time using secondary Y-axis
Clustered bar chart showing stockout frequency by region
Clustered bar chart comparing delivered quantity vs units sold by category

All 4 pages include interactive slicers for Year, Category, Brand, Region and Channel.
